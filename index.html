Mission & Scope

Mission. The IEEE Computer Society Israel Chapter advances the theory and practice of computing by fostering a vibrant community that spans academia and industry. We promote excellence in research, education, and professional development through technical events, knowledge exchange, and collaboration.

Scope. Our activities cover computer architecture and systems, hardware/software co-design, AI and machine learning, programming models and compilers, security and privacy, and emerging application domains. We highlight local innovation while engaging with the global IEEE CS community.

Objectives. (i) Curate high-quality talks and workshops; (ii) Enable student engagement and career development; (iii) Catalyze academia–industry partnerships; (iv) Support standards, open science, and responsible computing.

Chapter Highlights

  • Webinars. Monthly talks by leaders from academia and industry.
  • Workshops. Thematic half-day events co-located with major conferences.
  • Student Programs. Mentoring, poster sessions, and skills clinics.
  • Community. Networking meetups and cross-chapter collaborations.
